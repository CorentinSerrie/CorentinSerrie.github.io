Background

I'm a student in Copenhagen, currently pursuing the Digital Media Engineering master of DTU in Copenhagen, after having studied at Central Paris in France. I will graduate in summer 2020, after having finished my master thesis, focusing on the process of open development in game creation.

I started really getting into game development in my gap year in 2017, when after 6 month developing prototypes for various companies with other students of the Digital Tech Year, I joined Ubisoft Montreal for an internship. This was a really good oportunity to get insight into this industry, and got me craving for more. I then integrated DTU in hopes of joining the DADIU program, which I did in fall 2018 as a lead programmer. This has been my best experience so far, and I hope to get to work on similar projects in the future. In the meantime, I am finishing my studies while working part-time developing an app for R2N.
